## 1. Introduction
The Food Delivery App is a convenient way to order food from a variety of restaurants in your area. It connects you with local eateries, allowing you to explore menus, place orders, and have food delivered right to your doorstep. With an intuitive user interface and a wide selection of cuisines, the app aims to provide you with a delightful and hassle-free food ordering experience.

## 2. Getting Started
### Creating an Account
To use the Food Delivery App, you'll need to create an account. Follow these steps to get started:
1. Download and install the Food Delivery App from your device's app store.
2. Launch the app and tap on the "Sign Up" or "Create an Account" button.
3. Fill in the required information, such as your name, email address, and phone number.
4. Choose a secure password for your account.
5. Agree to the terms of service and privacy policy, then tap on the "Sign Up" button.
6. You may receive a verification email or SMS to verify your account. Follow the instructions to complete the verification process.

### Logging In
Once you have created an account, you can log in to access the app's features:
1. Open the Food Delivery App on your device.
2. Tap on the "Log In" or "Sign In" button.
3. Enter your registered email address or phone number and the password you set during account creation.
4. Tap on the "Log In" button.

### Browsing Restaurants
After logging in, you can start browsing restaurants in your area:
1. On the app's home screen, you'll see a list of nearby restaurants or popular options. You can also use the search bar to find specific restaurants.
2. Tap on a restaurant to view its menu, ratings, reviews, and other details.
3. Explore different categories or cuisines to discover more options.
4. You can apply filters such as price range, dietary preferences, or delivery time to narrow down your choices.

## 3. Placing an Order
### Searching for a Restaurant
To find a specific restaurant or cuisine:
1. Tap on the search bar at the top of the screen.
2. Enter the restaurant name, cuisine type, or any relevant keywords.
3. Browse the search results and tap on a restaurant to view its menu and details.

### Selecting Items
Once you've chosen a restaurant, you can browse its menu and select items to add to your order:
1. Tap on the restaurant to view its menu.
2. Explore different categories or swipe through the available options.
3. Tap on an item to see

 its description, price, and any customization options.
4. Select the desired quantity or portion size.
5. If available, choose any customizations (e.g., toppings, condiments) for the item.

### Customizing Your Order
Many restaurants allow customization of their menu items. To customize your order:
1. Tap on the item you wish to customize.
2. Review the available customization options.
3. Select your preferences (e.g., spice level, cooking instructions).
4. Tap on the "Add to Order" or "Done" button to save the customizations.

### Adding to Cart
After selecting your desired items, you can add them to your cart:
1. Tap on the "Add to Cart" or "Order Now" button on the item screen.
2. Review the items in your cart, including quantities and prices.
3. You can modify the quantity or remove items from the cart if needed.
4. Proceed to the checkout when you're ready.

### Placing the Order
To complete your order:
1. Tap on the "Checkout" or "Place Order" button in your cart.
2. Review your order details, including the items, quantities, and delivery address.
3. Select your preferred payment method (covered in Section 5).
4. If applicable, enter any coupons or discounts to be applied.
5. Confirm the order by tapping on the "Place Order" or "Confirm" button.
6. You will receive an order confirmation with an estimated delivery time.

## 4. Managing Your Orders
### Tracking Your Order
After placing an order, you can track its status and delivery progress:
1. Navigate to the "Orders" or "Order History" section in the app.
2. Find the specific order you want to track.
3. Tap on the order to view its details, including the delivery status.
4. Some restaurants may provide real-time tracking or notifications to keep you updated on the progress.

### Modifying or Canceling an Order
If you need to modify or cancel an order:
1. Access your order history in the app.
2. Find the order you wish to modify or cancel.
3. If the order is still being prepared or hasn't been dispatched, you can usually make changes or cancel it directly within the app.
4. Follow the provided options or contact customer support for assistance if necessary.

## 5. Payment and Checkout
### Payment Methods
The Food Delivery App supports various payment methods, which may include:
- Credit or debit cards
- Digital wallets (e.g., Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Cash on delivery (if available)
- In-app wallet or credits (if applicable)

### Applying Coupons or Discounts
If you have a coupon or discount code:
1. Proceed to the checkout page.
2. Look for a "Apply Coupon" or "Add Discount Code" option.
3. Enter the code exactly as provided, taking care of any letter case or spacing.
4. Tap on the "Apply" or "Add" button.
5. The discount will be applied to your order total, if valid.

### Reviewing the Order Total
Before confirming your order, review the order total, which includes the items, taxes, delivery fees (if applicable), and any discounts or promotions. Ensure that the total amount is accurate and acceptable before proceeding with the payment.

## 6. Account Settings
### Managing Your Profile
To manage your profile and account information:
1. Access the "Account" or "Profile" section in the app's navigation menu.
2. You can update your name, email address, phone number, and profile picture if desired.
3. Save any changes by tapping on the "Save" or "Update" button.

### Delivery Address Management
To add, edit, or

 delete delivery addresses:
1. Go to the "Address" or "Delivery Settings" section in the app.
2. Tap on "Add Address" or a similar option to add a new address.
3. Enter the necessary details, such as street name, building number, and contact information.
4. Save the address to use it for future orders.
5. To edit or delete an existing address, find the address in the list and use the provided options.

### Preferences and Notifications
The app may offer preferences and notification settings to enhance your experience:
1. Access the "Settings" or "Preferences" section in the app.
2. Configure your preferences for notifications, such as order updates, promotions, or restaurant recommendations.
3. You may be able to set dietary preferences, favorite cuisines, or delivery time preferences.

## 7. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
For answers to common questions or issues, consult the app's "FAQ" or "Help" section. It covers topics such as account management, payments, delivery policies, and more. The FAQ section provides quick solutions to common queries without needing to contact support.

## 8. Troubleshooting
If you encounter any issues while using the app, try the following steps:
1. Ensure that you have a stable internet connection.
2. Update the app to the latest version available in your device's app store.
3. Close the app completely and relaunch it.
4. Restart your device.
5. If the problem persists, consult the app's FAQ section or contact customer support.

## 9. Contact Support
If you require further assistance, you can reach out to the app's support team:
- Look for a "Contact Us," "Support," or "Help" option within the app's navigation menu.
- Use the provided contact methods such as email, phone, or live chat.
- Describe your issue or query clearly to receive prompt and accurate support.
